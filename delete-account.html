Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: 2026

Users can request deletion of their Nudge account and associated app data.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to oguzhanbodur23@gmail.com using the email address associated with your Nudge account.

What to Include

Please include the subject line Nudge Account Deletion Request and mention that you want your account and app data deleted.

Data That May Be Deleted

Account information, profile data, nutrition logs, workout logs, app preferences, and related user-generated data may be deleted where technically applicable.

Data That May Be Retained

Some limited records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup integrity. Retained data will not be used for active app personalization.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are reviewed and processed within a reasonable period after verification of account ownership.
