Nudge Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

Nudge is an AI-powered fitness and nutrition application designed to help users track their health data, workouts, and daily habits. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect

Nudge may collect the following types of data:

1. User Provided Data

- Profile information (age, gender, goals)
- Nutrition inputs (foods, calories, macros)
- Workout activity data

2. Device & Usage Data

- App usage interactions
- Device information (limited, for performance and debugging)

3. Health Data (Optional)

With your permission, Nudge may access health-related data via device integrations such as Huawei Health Kit:

Steps Calories Heart Rate Sleep

How We Use Data

Your data is used to:

- Generate personalized workout plans
- Provide nutrition tracking and insights
- Improve AI-based recommendations
- Enhance overall user experience

AI Processing

Nudge uses AI models to generate workout plans, recommendations, and insights. Data processed for AI features is limited to what is necessary to provide these services.

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using cloud services such as Firebase. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data may be processed by trusted third-party services (such as Firebase) strictly for application functionality.

User Control

- You can update or delete your data within the app.
- You can revoke permissions (e.g., health data) through your device settings.

Security

We implement standard security practices to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

Nudge is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Disclaimer

This application provides general fitness and nutrition guidance and does not replace professional medical advice.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
