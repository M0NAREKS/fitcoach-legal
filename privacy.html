Nudge Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Nudge is designed to provide general fitness and nutrition guidance. It is not a medical service and does not replace professional medical advice.

Overview

Nudge is an AI-powered fitness and nutrition application designed to help users track daily habits, nutrition, and workouts. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how users can control their information.

Data We Collect

Nudge may collect the following types of data depending on the features used by the user.

1. User Provided Data

Profile information such as age, fitness goals, nutrition goals, meals, calories, macro values, workout activity, and app preferences.

2. Account Data

If account features are used, Nudge may process authentication-related information such as email address and user identifier through Firebase Authentication.

3. Device and Usage Data

Limited technical and usage information may be collected to improve reliability, performance, debugging, and app experience.

4. Health Data Optional

With user permission, Nudge may access health-related data through device or wearable integrations, such as:

Steps Calories Heart Rate Sleep Activity Data

How We Use Data

Your data may be used to generate personalized workout plans, track nutrition progress, provide AI-based recommendations, improve app performance, maintain account functionality, and enhance user experience.

AI Processing

Nudge uses AI models to generate workout plans, recommendations, and insights. Data processed for AI features is limited to what is necessary to provide the requested functionality.

Data Storage

User data may be stored using cloud services such as Firebase. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers, such as Firebase or AI service providers, strictly to operate and improve the application.

User Control

Users can update or delete app data where supported by the application. Users can revoke device permissions, including health data permissions, through device settings.

Account and Data Deletion

Users may request account or data deletion by following the instructions on the Delete Account page or by contacting us at the email address below.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no digital system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Nudge is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
